My Philosophy and Approach to Wellness...

We provide quality, personalized care in a timely manner for a discerning patient population that is interested in health promotion, prevention and health potential maximization. Our goal continues to be your doctor of choice for your full spectrum primary care, understanding and acknowledging that healthcare is a team effort patient and doctor and that no one person has all the answers. With patients as the center of their own healthcare, our role is to encourage you to be as healthy as you can be, and get you to the appropriate specialists, when needed.

As a family practice physician with over 30 years experience and a broad range of interests including geriatrics/older adult medicine and experience in military medicine/occupational medicine as an Air Force Flight Surgeon, serving the greater Southeast Michigan area including Ann Arbor, Ypsilanti and Washtenaw County, we provide personalized healthcare. My MDVIP-affiliated practice offers services, including comprehensive, advanced health screenings and diagnostic tests, that go far beyond those found in concierge medicine practices. I am affiliated with both St. Joseph Mercy Hospital Ann Arbor and The University of Michigan Health System, working closely with their outpatient and inpatient specialists to enhance and improve your care. I support a holistic, integrative approach to health including diet, exercise and alternative medical care including massage, yoga and herbal medication in addition to traditional medical care.
